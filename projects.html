Project: Digital clock

type: Electronics and CAD design project

Built: January 2025

I used an Arduino uno to build a digital clock. I first started by drawing some rough designs, where I labled where I wanted everything to be placed. I then hopped onto OnShape where I 3D modeled my design taking elements from the two designs and making one. I then generated an Engineering drawing and set up the orthographic views for measurements. I printed it out and used them as a template, where I transferred them to cardboard and cut them out. I then worked on the electronics. I first planned out the code on a sheet of paper and then excecuted my code. I built my circuit using jumper cables, an Arduino uno and an LCD screen.
